Blesst Chest

photo by Jason Quigley

Blesst Chest make inspired, bizzaro instrumental tunes that are equal parts 60s Psych Rock, 70's Prog Rock, 80s post punk and 90's post rock. The music is wildly rich and inventive and exists in an elusive landscape that defies easy classification. Their debut record Wish We Were There was engineered by Gary Jarman and mastered by Dave Fulton in Portland, OR.
